Product Manager

Sep 2022 - present

I am part of the Integration squad at Selectra and our mission is to enable engineering squads to achieve more. We do so by providing them with simple and standardized APIs we build ourselves, abstracting the complexity and unreliability of external APIs in the energy industry. I directly collaborate with technical clients and partners, such as Enedis or GRDF, to obtain access to APIs, improve them by raising bugs, and build a strong relationship to improve the integration between our systems.

I am responsible for defining and measuring success for our APIs, which I then translate into KPIs that I track every day to assess our impact. I make sure everyone understands the vision, that the squad is in direct contact with stakeholders, and that there is a short feedback loop in terms of frequent delivery to our users.

I continuously meet with our users to emphasize with them and to understand their motivations by asking "why". This helps me identify a theme and define a clear problem to be solved, which I translate into user stories with acceptance criteria, and hypotheses to be tested. When the problem requires a creative solution, I use "How might we" statements and the jobs-to-be-done framework and I invite the whole team to ideate together.

I then build, refine, and prioritize our backlog to best achieve our goals and deliver value rapidly. The hardest part of my job is saying "no", deciding what not to build and assuming the consequences of that decision. I seek feedback from our users as early as possible with rapid testing of ideas. I work closely with software engineers and drive story mapping workshops.
